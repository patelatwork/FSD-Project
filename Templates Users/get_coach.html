Frequently Asked Questions

How are coaches matched with clients?

We match coaches based on your specific goals, experience level, and personal preferences. You'll be paired with a coach who specializes in the areas that align with your fitness journey.

How often will I communicate with my coach?

Communication frequency depends on your plan. Basic plans include weekly check-ins, Premium plans include bi-weekly video calls and messaging support, and Elite plans feature weekly video calls and priority support.

Can I switch coaches if we're not a good fit?

Absolutely. If you feel your coach isn't the right match within the first 14 days, we'll happily pair you with a different coach at no additional cost.

Do I need special equipment for the workouts?

Not necessarily. Your coach will design your program based on the equipment you have access to, whether it's a fully equipped gym or minimal home equipment.

Are the nutrition plans customizable for dietary restrictions?

Yes, all nutrition plans are fully customizable to accommodate allergies, intolerances, preferences, and dietary restrictions such as vegetarian, vegan, keto, etc.

Can I pause my coaching plan?

Yes, you can pause your plan for up to 30 days per year without losing any coaching time. This is particularly helpful during vacations or busy periods.
