Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get tested?

We recommend comprehensive testing every 3-6 months for active individuals. Specific tests may be recommended more frequently based on your training program and goals.

Do I need to fast before tests?

Most tests require 8-12 hours of fasting for accurate results. Specific instructions will be provided when you book your appointment.

How do I interpret my results?

All test results include detailed explanations. Additionally, you can schedule a consultation with our health experts to review your results and get personalized recommendations.

Are the tests covered by insurance?

Many of our tests are eligible for insurance coverage. We recommend checking with your provider before booking. We also offer flexible payment options for all our services.
